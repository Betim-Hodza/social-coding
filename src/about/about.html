About Us

We're Mobi, an app development club just for you. We're a community of developer friends that are here to help you code and have fun!

Membership

All of our events are completely free, so membership is free. We want to make sure no one is excluded in our events. If you would like to additionally support Mobi and its events, you can purchase a membership package for a tshirt.

Philosophy

We’re a community-first group. You don’t have to be an engineer to code, and you don’t have to be super smart! Everyone can build! We’re just here to help you make it real.

Events

We have bunch of cool events happening throughout the semester, one of them being social coding. Bring your friends! You can particpate in our events just by coming in person!
